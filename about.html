About Us – PawHub

PawHub is a pet care website designed to help pet owners find the best products, services, and adoption opportunities for their pets in one place. The platform provides information about pet accessories, food products, grooming services, and heartwarming adoption stories. Our goal is to create a convenient and user-friendly space where pet lovers can explore useful resources for taking better care of their pets and ensuring their well-being.

The main aim of this website is to promote responsible pet ownership and make pet care easier by providing reliable information and services in a single platform. PawHub helps users discover quality pet products, learn about grooming services for maintaining pet hygiene, and explore adoption stories that encourage people to give animals a loving home.

This website has been developed as part of an academic project by Aditya Singh. It demonstrates the use of web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create an interactive and visually appealing pet care platform.
